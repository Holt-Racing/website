Introduction

Once upon a Corona, there was a significant amount of boredom in the air. Barred from doing any practical research and armed with a significant amount of electronics expertise in part due to a decade of accidentally burning himself and working twenty-four seven, Oliver Holt founded Holt Racing. Founded in Spring 2020. Its mission... to be determined. Location of headquarters... his downstairs office in his parents' house in Surrey (because of course it had to be in Surrey).

Holt Racing combines his love of electronics and the (lack of) protocol and practicalities in electric motorsport. (That image on the left could be a particularly handsome photograph, by the way.)
